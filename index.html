Clock-In & Clock-Out Guide

Learn how to use the new system and OneApp for accurate attendance tracking.

Step-by-Step Guide

Follow these steps to clock in and out using the new system:

  • Step 1: When you arrive, scan your ID card at the terminal to Clock-In.
  • Step 2: Before you leave, scan your card again to Clock-Out.
  • Step 3: Ensure your hours are logged accurately for salary processing.

Using the OneApp

You can also clock in and out using the OneApp on your mobile device. Here's how:

  • Download and log in to the OneApp.
  • Tap the Clock-In button when you arrive.
  • Tap the Clock-Out button when you leave.

Note: The OneApp works alongside the terminal punch cards, giving you flexibility.

Geographical Range Reminder

To use the OneApp for clocking in and out, you must be within the eligible geographical range. If you're outside this range, use the terminal punch cards.
